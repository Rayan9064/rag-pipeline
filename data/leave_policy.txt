Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of PTO per month, up to a maximum annual accrual of 18 days.
Employees can carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year.
Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on January 31.

PTO requests for 3 or more consecutive days should be submitted at least 10 business days in advance.
Manager approval is required before booking travel.
